The Jiangsu Provincial Market Supervision Administration has proposed a series of measures to assist enterprises in difficulty and stabilize the economic situation. This includes implementing the "Su Zheng 40" and "Su Zheng Ban 22" policies, issuing 12 measures to help enterprises alleviate difficulties and stabilize enterprises and strengthen chains, and subsequently, 13 prefecture-level cities and 31 county (city, district) bureaus have also introduced supporting measures and are actively implementing them. The measures cover various aspects such as enterprise needs visits, market access, inclusive and prudent supervision, price and fee supervision, fee reduction and exemption, and quality assistance.
